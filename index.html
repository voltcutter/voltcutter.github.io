Games

Human Experiment

Human experiment is a game where you experiment on humans and make contraptions. For example, you can pump someone's head full of blood and their head will explode! This is a very interesting game that was definitely not made for psychopaths.


Don't Let Them See You

Don't Let Them See You is a comedic horror game. Run around in an industrial pipe factory and avoid being seen by the iconic antagonist, Among Us Figure. If the Among Us Figure or any of its friends see you, well prepare to be sent to the Hall of Infinite Pain.
